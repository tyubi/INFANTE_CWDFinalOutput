Application Development Management

As an Application Development Manager, you will be responsible for overseeing the design, development, and implementation of software applications within our organization. You will lead a team of developers and engineers, guiding them through the entire software development lifecycle, from requirements gathering to deployment.

Contact Center Services NC II

Contact Center Services NC II is a vocational course that prepares individuals for careers in the contact center industry. Graduates of this program are equipped with the necessary skills and knowledge to provide customer service and support through various communication channels, including phone calls, emails, chat, and social media platforms.

Creative Web Design

Creative web design involves the conceptualization, creation, and implementation of visually appealing and engaging websites. It combines elements of graphic design, user interface (UI) design, and user experience (UX) design to deliver websites that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also functional and user-friendly.

Event Management NC III

Event Management NC III is a vocational course designed to equip individuals with the knowledge, skills, and competencies needed to plan, organize, and execute various types of events effectively. This program covers the entire event management process, from conceptualization and planning to implementation, coordination, and evaluation.
