INTERNS

An intern is a temporary employee who works within an organization to gain practical experience and exposure to a particular industry or field. Interns typically assist with various tasks and projects under the guidance of experienced professionals, providing support in areas such as research, data analysis, administrative duties, and project management.

WEB DEVELOPERS

Web developers are skilled professionals responsible for designing, building, and maintaining websites and web applications. They work with programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create user-friendly, visually appealing, and functional websites that meet client or organizational needs. Web developers collaborate with designers, clients, and other team members to understand project requirements, develop technical solutions, and ensure the smooth operation of websites across various devices and browsers.

INSTRUCTORS

Instructors are individuals who teach and guide students in various subjects or skills. They can work in a wide range of settings, including schools, colleges, universities, vocational training centers, corporate training programs, and online platforms. Instructors are experts in their respective fields, possessing in-depth knowledge and practical experience that they share with their students. They create lesson plans, deliver lectures or training sessions, facilitate discussions, provide feedback on assignments, and assess student performance.
